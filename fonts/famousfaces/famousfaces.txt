Famous Faces Truetype Font for Windows (version 1)

2007 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

http://www.iconian.com

This is a dingbats fonts.  Upper and lower case A-Z (as well as numerals and extra punctuation) contain over 80 images of famous faces - founding fathers, politicians, celebrities, even a few fictional people. A short, incomplete list of some of the people in this font: George Washington, Abraham Lincoln, Andrew Jackson, John F. Kennedy, Johnny Cocharan, Johnny Carson, Charlie Chaplin, Princess Diana, Scarfcace, Elvis, James Dean, Marilyn Monroe, Babe Ruth, Martin Luther King Jr., Uncle Sam, Clint Eastwood, Oprah Winfrey and many others.

Use of a character map program, such as Windows' Character Map, will be very helpful in exploring and using this font.
